Hoang Anh Vu is a student at Boston University, currently double majoring in Computer Science and Psychology. His passion for Computer Science is fueled by his love for coding, while his interest in Psychology stems from a desire to better understand human behavior. Recognizing the importance of making senior management better, while also aligning with his academic interests, he decided to join forces in the projects. Beyond academics, he enjoys playing video games, playing with the piano despite not being particularly skilled, watching movies, and learning many disciplines from science to history.
Brandon Nguyen
Brandon is an avid basketballer pursuing a major in Computer Science and minor in Mathematics. He was inspired by his time volunteering at his local hospice center after witnessing the disorganized workflows of managing volunteers and seniors. Some of his favorite memories include reading and writing to seniors and often times, playing board games with them. Brandon is motivated by the impact that he can have on others, and hopes to leverage technology to achieve this.
Felix Nguyen
Felix Nguyen, a Computer Science major at California East Bay University, combines his passion for technology with a strong sense of community. His interests in basketball and football have taught him discipline and strategy, while his experiences caring for senior family members highlighted the value of organization. During the COVID pandemic, Felix developed a keen interest in coding, particularly in building websites and simple games. Inspired by these experiences, he aims to leverage cutting-edge technology to create efficient management tools that empower individuals and improve lives.
