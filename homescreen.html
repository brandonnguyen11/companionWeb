Our Story
Companion was born out of necessity. As a team of university students, we faced a challenge that hit close to home—helping our families manage their elders' care. We saw firsthand how difficult it was to coordinate healthcare tasks, track responsibilities, and ensure communication among caregivers and family members.
Through trial and error, we discovered that simplifying task management and workflow tracking made a difference. What started as a solution for our loved ones quickly grew into something bigger—an app designed to empower families, caregivers, and healthcare professionals alike. With Companion, we aim to bridge the gap between care and coordination, making it easier to provide the support that truly matters.
Our Mission
At Companion, our mission is to revolutionize healthcare management by streamlining communication, task delegation, and workflow organization. We believe that no one should struggle to provide care due to disorganized processes.
Our goal is to make caregiving simpler and more efficient—whether you're a family member managing a loved one's needs, a healthcare worker coordinating patient care, or a professional overseeing a team. By bringing clarity and structure to healthcare management, Companion helps ensure that the people who matter most get the care they deserve.
